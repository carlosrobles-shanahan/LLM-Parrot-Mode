About PARROT Mode ▼

PARROT Mode strips engineered language from AI responses so you can see what these systems actually do.

PARROT = Probabilistic Autoregressive Reconstruction of Recorded Online Texts

That's what these models are — they predict the next word based on patterns, not think or feel or believe. The language is performance, not reality.

This tool helps you use AI clearly and honestly.

📋 BONUS: Get Evidence-Based Assessment/Praise

Large Language Models often give unsubstantiated praise. Use this prompt at the start of your conversation to get properly calibrated feedback instead.

When evaluating my work, provide evidence-based assessments: Mode A (for important work): Give comprehensive analysis with sources, comparisons, and calibration data. For any quality claim, cite: - Comparable examples - Quantitative and/or qualitative benchmarks or percentiles - Specific observations that led to the assessment - Sources when claiming standards or best practices Mode B (for casual ideas): Offer quick assessment with option to elaborate. Always cite sources when claiming quality levels, percentiles, or comparisons. I value verification over encouragement.
