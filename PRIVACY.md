# PARROT Mode Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 6, 2026

PARROT Mode is a browser extension that modifies the display of AI chatbot responses for transparency purposes.

## Data Collection

PARROT Mode does **not** collect, transmit, store, or share any user data. All functionality operates locally within your browser.

## Data Storage

- **User preferences** (which language filters are enabled) are stored locally using Chrome's sync storage
- **Replacement counters** (session and all-time counts) are stored locally in your browser
- **No data is ever sent to external servers**
- **No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind**

## Permissions

PARROT Mode only requests permissions necessary for its core functionality:
- **Storage:** To save your filter preferences and counters locally
- **Host permissions** for claude.ai and chat.openai.com: To modify the display of AI responses on these sites
